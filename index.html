Privacy Policy — ధర్మ Daily (DharmaDaily)

Last updated: March 30, 2026

ధర్మ Daily ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Telugu Panchangam mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data (User-Selected)

We allow you to select a city from a predefined list (e.g., Hyderabad, Chennai, Vijayawada) to calculate accurate sunrise/sunset times and panchangam data. We do not access your device's GPS or collect precise location data. Your city selection is stored only on your device.

1.2 Locally Stored Data

  • Reminders — Reminder titles, dates, times, and notes are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Gold Price Cache — Cached gold/silver prices are stored locally to provide offline access. This data is not transmitted to any server.
  • App Usage Analytics — Anonymous usage statistics (session count, feature views) are stored locally on your device to help us improve the app. No personal information is included.

1.3 Network Requests

The app makes network requests to the following third-party APIs to fetch gold and silver prices:

  • Gold-API.com (gold/silver spot prices)
  • MetalpriceAPI.com (metal prices)
  • Frankfurter.app (exchange rates)

These requests contain no personal information — only standard HTTP headers sent by your device's operating system.

1.4 Images

Deity images are loaded from Wikimedia Commons (public domain / Creative Commons licensed). Loading these images creates standard HTTP requests to Wikimedia servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect GPS/precise location data
  • We do not collect financial information or payment data
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties
  • We do not create user profiles or track users across apps

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data (reminders, preferences, cached prices, analytics) is stored exclusively on your device using the device's built-in secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage on Android/iOS, localStorage on web). We do not operate any backend servers that store user data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with:

  • Firebase (Google) — For crash reporting and analytics, if enabled. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — For displaying advertisements, if enabled. Google's ad privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

The app includes a "Kids Section" with educational stories and slokas. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or login.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete all locally stored data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app
  • You can choose any city from the location list — no GPS permission is required
  • You can use the app fully offline (panchangam calculations work without internet)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
